Welcome to the MCA Department Achievement Tracker Portal - VICTOLOG

About the Website

The MCA Department Achievement Tracker Portal - VICTOLOG allows students to record and track their achievements, including extracurricular activities, placement records, and workshop achievements. This portal enables students to showcase their accomplishments and share them with the department.

Student Achievements

Students can add their academic and extracurricular achievements, such as awards, publications, and leadership roles.

Placement Records

Record your job placements, internship experiences, and career milestones for prospective employers to view.

Workshop Achievements

Document your participation in workshops, seminars, and coding competitions to build a comprehensive portfolio.
